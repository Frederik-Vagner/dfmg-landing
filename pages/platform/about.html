OUR STORY

From spreadsheets to 40+ modules

DFMG started as a facility service company in Denmark. Like every small service business, we managed schedules in spreadsheets, tracked invoices in one system, payroll in another, and client communication through a mix of email and phone calls.

We started building tools to solve our own problems. A task scheduler. An invoice generator. A time tracker. One tool led to another, and before we knew it, we had built a complete platform that runs every aspect of our operation.

Today, we use DFMG Platform to run our own cleaning business — and we're making it available to other Danish service companies who face the same challenges we did.

PHILOSOPHY
What we believe

Practitioner-built

Every feature exists because we needed it in our own business. We don't build based on market research — we build based on daily operational reality.

Danish-first

We're not a US product with Danish compliance bolted on. eIndkomst, SKAT, Danish holiday law, e-Faktura, NemKonto — it's all native, not an afterthought.

One connected system

When an employee logs hours, it flows into invoicing, payroll, profitability analytics, and client reports. No manual re-entry, no data silos, no sync issues.

TECHNOLOGY

Modern, secure, scalable

  • Multi-tenant architecture — each company gets its own isolated database schema
  • EU-hosted — all data stays within the European Union
  • GDPR compliant — privacy by design, data processing agreements included
  • Real-time — ASGI server with WebSocket support for live updates
  • Mobile-ready — employees access their tasks, schedule, and reports from any device
  • Modular — enable only the modules you need, disable what you don't
