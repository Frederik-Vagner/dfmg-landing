FEATURES

40+ modules designed for service companies

Everything you need to run operations, manage people, handle finances, and grow your business — in one connected platform.

The daily engine of your business

Schedule tasks, track time, manage quality, and keep your entire operation visible in real time. Your employees check in via their phones, and you see it all on the dashboard — who's where, what's done, what's pending.

Every client, every location, every detail

Manage client information, addresses, contact persons, and location-specific guides that tell your team exactly how to service each site. Clients get their own portal to see upcoming tasks, invoices, photos, and floor plans.

Employee Management

From hiring to daily performance

Employees get their own portal with tasks, schedules, salary slips, and holiday requests. Managers track performance through an improvement system with tests, scoring, and incident tracking. The org diagram shows your entire structure at a glance.

Financial Suite

Complete accounting built for Danish companies

Full double-entry accounting with Danish chart of accounts, automated invoicing with e-Faktura support, bank reconciliation, BankConnect integration for direct bank payments, and VAT reporting ready for SKAT. Generate income statements and balance sheets with one click.

Chart of Accounts Invoicing e-Faktura Credit Notes Bank Reconciliation BankConnect Vendor Bills VAT Reports Income Statement Balance Sheet Journal Entries

Payroll

Danish payroll from hours to payslip

Create payroll runs from tracked hours, calculate taxes and deductions, generate payslips, report to eIndkomst/SKAT, and export bank payment files — all without leaving the platform. Holiday pay and pension calculations follow Danish law automatically.

Track every item, from warehouse to client site

Manage inventory across multiple storage locations, create purchase orders to suppliers, track stock levels, and build loadout templates for what each team needs on the road. Employees report usage directly from the task view.
