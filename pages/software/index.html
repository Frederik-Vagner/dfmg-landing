Danish-first
eIndkomst, SKAT, e-Faktura, Danish holiday law, NemKonto — compliance built in from day one, not bolted on like international tools.
Modular

MODULES
One platform, every part of your business
Six capability areas, 40+ modules — replacing spreadsheets, WhatsApp groups, and disconnected tools.
Core Operations
Schedule jobs, track time, manage quality inspections, and see who's where — all in real time from your phone or desktop.
Client Management

Employee Management
